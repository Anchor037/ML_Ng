3. Linear regression
   - Hypothesis function: $h_\theta(x) = \theta_0 + \theta_1x$
   - Cost function: $J(\theta_0,\theta_1) = \frac{1}{2m}\sum_{i = 1}^{m}(h_\theta(x^{(i)}) - y^{(i)})^2$   (Squared error function)
   - Goal: $\underset{\theta_0, \theta_1}{minimize} J(\theta_0, \theta_1)$
